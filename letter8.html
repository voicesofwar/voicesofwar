Analysis

These letters from Michele show a truer side of the average man that was sent to the front, he uses a very basic grammar and makes frequent mistakes with accents, double letters and sometimes uses terms of his dialect. Punctuation is almost non present; there Is also a frequent use of specific formulas. Beside the frequent errors with the accents that are shown in the transcribed texts, there are many errors that should be analysed for a proper study and to facilitate the comprehension of the text for non-italian users.

One term is used in the final letter Lazzaroni which is not a frequently used term in italian which indicates an evil person but also a lazy person.Michele also as is shown in the letters, tends to curve the letters while he is writing, this is a signal (beside the limitation of writing probably without a support) of a person who isn’t used to writing a lot, making the final letter even more powerful and heart-breaking as it shows a simple man forced to the front who only wished to return back home.


Brief Analysis and Context of the War

After the assassination of Francis Ferdinand Hasburg- Este Europe plunged into chaos. Old grudges, dreams to bring back empires and old alliances were climaxed in an instant. After the declaration of war of the Austro Hungaric towards Serbia, Germany declared war to Russia who mobilised the army. Soon all of Europe was brought into a fierce conflict that took so many men away from their homes. After a stalemate in central Europe against the axis the English decided to invade Gallipoli in Turkey for the precious resources that were present in the country, oil. Italy entered the war due to a nationalistic sentiment and old grudges against the Austrian empire who occupied some areas that were considered by the italians part of the Madre patria.
