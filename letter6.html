Letters of Umberto Lombardo Radice to his brother Giuseppe Lombardo Radice - 2

Umberto Lombardo Radice, born in Catania, came from a family of office workers, had six brothers. Decided to join the front line at the declaration of the war. He fought along the Carsic border against the Austro Hungarians.

Analysis

The grammar used by Umberto is perfect as is his use of punctuation. In the last letter he uses grand terms to address how he scaled the ranks of the army. He mentions that he wants to be transferred either to Somalia or Eritrea, even asking for a recommendation from the ministry of war, this is a clear sign that his family came from a well-known family that had contacts in the ministry.
