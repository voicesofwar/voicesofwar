10/12/1915

Dearest sister Delfina I came to know that mom is at your house so I write you Delfina, I am fine and so I hope for you and our dear mom and your dear children Delfina tell mom () and that she eats without malincony since at the present I wont run into danger. For now receive my affectionate regards to you and our dear mom and a thousand kisses to your dear children. 

To miss Longhi Delfina Borgarello, Torre del Marghero, Parma
